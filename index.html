Why they are Important?

Interdomain Application

As per observation of the dataset most of the product description does not have a model name.  This leads to difficulty for a customer who is purchasing the expected product where it has every detail without product name or model name. Hence to solve this semantic challenge we are going to classify the electronic products based on model name, so that if a customer searches with model name he can get products of that particular model.

Keyword Filteration

The semantic filtering proves to be the best way to filter the unrelated services and avoid filtering the relevant services. When user searches particular word it filters that keyword and gives the output. keyword analysis of text is the basis of the filtering. Keyword filtering is done by using SPARQL language in semantic web.
